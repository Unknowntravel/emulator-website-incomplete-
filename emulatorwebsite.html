Getting ROMs Legally

Retro Game Preservation

We believe in preserving the history of video games. While emulators are legal, obtaining ROMs without permission can be a legal gray area. Here are some tips to help you get ROMs legally:

  • Support retro game preservation efforts by purchasing original cartridges or discs.
  • Check if the game publisher or developer offers official ROM downloads.
  • Explore retro gaming marketplaces and services that provide legal ROM access.
  • Seek out abandonware websites that host ROMs for games no longer commercially available.
  • Note: You may need to redump old games to be able to play them on your emulator, as some ROMs available online may not work properly.
